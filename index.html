Is TaxBridge really free forever?
+
Yes — completely free. Unlimited invoices, unlimited expense tracking, unlimited FIRS-ready reports, forever. We built TaxBridge because every Nigerian business deserves professional financial tools, not just companies with big budgets.
Do I need accounting knowledge to use it?
+
Not at all. TaxBridge is built for business owners, not accountants. VAT calculates automatically. Tax rates are pre-configured to FIRS standards. If you can send a WhatsApp message, you can use TaxBridge. We guide you through every step.
What happens when there's no internet?
+
Everything still works. Create invoices, scan receipts, record expenses, view reports — all completely offline using local storage. When you reconnect (even days later), TaxBridge syncs everything automatically. You will never lose any data.
Is TaxBridge really NRS 2026 and FIRS compliant?
+
Yes. Invoices are submitted via DigiTax — a NITDA-accredited Access Point Provider — in UBL 3.0 / Peppol BIS Billing 3.0 format as required by Nigeria's 2025 Tax Act. Every invoice gets a Cryptographic Stamp Identifier (CSID) and Invoice Reference Number (IRN) directly from FIRS.
Can my accountant see my records?
+
Yes. Share view-only access to the web dashboard. They see all invoices, expenses, and reports — everything needed to prepare your taxes — but cannot modify or delete anything. You stay in full control at all times.
What businesses is TaxBridge designed for?
+
Any Nigerian business that sends invoices or tracks expenses for tax purposes — fashion designers, caterers, consultants, retailers, traders, freelancers. If you do business in Nigeria, TaxBridge was built for you.
How secure is my financial data?
+
256-bit AES encryption for all sensitive fields (TIN, NIN). Immutable audit logs. NDPC compliant, WCAG 2.1 AA accessible. You own your data completely — export or delete everything at any time, no questions asked.
