Common questions

Everything you need to know about TaxBridge

Is TaxBridge really free?

Yes, TaxBridge is completely free to use. You can create unlimited invoices, track all your expenses, and generate tax reports without paying anything. We believe every Nigerian business deserves access to professional tools.

Do I need accounting knowledge to use it?

Not at all. TaxBridge is designed for business owners, not accountants. We guide you through everything step-by-step in simple language. VAT and other calculations happen automatically.

Does it work without internet?

Yes! You can create invoices, record expenses, and view reports completely offline. Everything syncs automatically when you connect to the internet.

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption to protect your data. Your information is stored securely and we never share it with anyone. You own your data and can export or delete it anytime.

Can my accountant access my records?

Yes. You can give your accountant view-only access to your dashboard. They can see all your records and generate reports, but they can't change anything without your permission.

What if I need help?

We're here to help! You can reach us through in-app support, email at support@taxbridge.ng, or WhatsApp. We respond within 24 hours, usually much faster.
