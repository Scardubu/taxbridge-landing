Features

Everything you need to run a compliant Nigerian business

Offline-first, low-data, and regulation-aware — features built to work where you work.

Snap receipts, claim deductions

AI extracts merchant, amount, date and VAT from messy receipts and auto-categorises expenses — so you never miss a deduction.

FIRS-ready invoices in 30s

Generate NRS-compliant invoices with QR and required stamps. Share instantly — customers can pay from the invoice link.

Never miss a deadline

TaxBridge updates VAT, PAYE, CIT automatically so you always know what’s due and when — file with a single click.

Works offline — syncs when online

Create invoices, scan receipts and track expenses even without data. Everything syncs once you reconnect.

Get paid faster

Paystack, Flutterwave and Remita integrations let customers pay from invoice links — money lands in your account quickly.

Free, open source — you control data

No lock-in. Audit the code on GitHub. Your data stays encrypted on device and in transit — we do not hold your financial records.
