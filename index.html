Questions you probably have

Straight answers about how TaxBridge works

Is TaxBridge really free forever?

Yes, completely free. Create unlimited invoices, track all your expenses, and generate unlimited tax reports without ever paying. We built TaxBridge because we believe every Nigerian business deserves professional tools, not just big companies with big budgets.

Do I need to understand accounting?

Not at all. TaxBridge is built for business owners, not accountants. We explain everything in simple language. VAT calculates automatically. If you can send a WhatsApp message, you can use TaxBridge. We guide you through every step.

What happens when there's no internet?

Everything still works. Create invoices, record expenses, scan receipts, view your reports — all completely offline. When you connect to the internet again (even days later), everything syncs automatically. You'll never lose anything.

How secure is my financial information?

We use bank-level encryption to protect your data — the same security standard as Nigerian online banking. Your information is stored securely and we never share it with anyone. You own your data and can export or delete everything anytime you want.

Can my accountant access my records?

Yes. You can give your accountant view-only access to your web dashboard. They'll see all your invoices, expenses, and reports — everything they need to prepare your taxes — but they can't change or delete anything without your permission.

Does TaxBridge work on my phone?

TaxBridge works on any Android phone running version 8.0 or newer (most phones from 2018 onwards). The web dashboard works on any phone, tablet, or computer with an internet browser — no download needed.

What if I already have invoices from before?

You can start using TaxBridge today and go forward. If you want to bring in old records, you can manually add past invoices or take photos of old receipts to get everything in one place. Many users start fresh and use it for tax preparation going forward.

What if I need help or get stuck?

We're here to help. Contact us through the app support chat, email support@taxbridge.ng, or message us on WhatsApp. We respond within 24 hours, usually much faster. We want you to succeed.

Will this work for my type of business?

If you send invoices or need to track business expenses for tax purposes, TaxBridge will work for you. We're used by fashion designers, caterers, consultants, traders, tech freelancers, small retailers, and many other Nigerian businesses.
