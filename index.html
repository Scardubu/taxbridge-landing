Common Questions

Can't find your answer here? Email our team — we reply within one business day.

For Business Owners & Finance Managers
What happens to my business if I miss the July 2026 deadline? +
After July 1, 2026, invoices that have not been cleared through the NRS system will be considered invalid under the regulation. This means your customers may be unable to use them for tax deductions, and your business could be exposed to penalties. Additionally, buyers may begin to insist on NRS-compliant invoices. Starting early gives you enough time to test thoroughly before the deadline.
Does my business need to apply to the government separately? +
No. TaxBridge holds the official government Access Point Provider licence, which means your business operates under our authorisation. You do not need to make any government applications or appointments — just sign up with us and you are covered.
How long does it actually take to get set up? +
Most businesses complete their setup and are issuing compliant invoices within 1 to 5 working days. Businesses with more complex systems or multiple branches may take longer. We offer free onboarding support and can recommend integration partners if your team needs technical assistance.
Is my business data safe with TaxBridge? +
Yes. All invoice data is encrypted in transit and stored securely on NCC-compliant servers based in Nigeria. Your data does not leave the country. We also provide complete audit logs so you always have a full record of every invoice submitted.
Does my team need training to use TaxBridge? +
No. TaxBridge works invisibly in the background. Your staff keep using the same software they use today — TaxBridge connects to it and handles compliance automatically. Your finance team gets access to a simple dashboard to monitor invoice status and download records.
For Developers & Technical Teams
What are the API rate limits and uptime guarantee? +
Sandbox: high test call allowance — contact us if you need a higher limit. Growth plan: up to 50,000 live invoices per month. Enterprise: custom volume pricing. Target SLA: 99.9% monthly uptime. Target P95 response time: under 200ms. Live status at status.taxbridge.ng.
What happens when NRS updates their API spec? +
We handle all NRS API changes internally. Your integration contract with TaxBridge is stable — you will never need to update your code because NRS changed their spec. We absorb that complexity on your behalf and communicate any impactful changes through our changelog.
Is there offline support for POS environments? +
Yes. Set mode: "offline" in the SDK config. Invoices queue locally and sync automatically via POST /v1/invoices/sync when connectivity is restored. Detailed docs available in the developer portal.
