Features

Built for How Nigerian
Businesses Actually Work

Low data. Unreliable power. Complex regulations. TaxBridge handles it all so you can focus on growing your business.

AI Receipt Scanning

Photograph any receipt. AI extracts merchant, date, amount, and VAT—categorised into 13 expense types automatically.

NRS-Compliant Invoices

Professional invoices with QR codes, FIRS IRN stamping, and mandatory tax IDs. Send via WhatsApp, email, or PDF.

6 Tax Calculators

PIT, VAT (7.5%), CIT, CGT, WHT, and PAYE—all calculated automatically as you transact. Never guess what you owe.

Offline-First Design

Everything works without internet. Local SQLite database syncs automatically when connectivity returns. Zero data loss.

Accept Payments

Paystack, Flutterwave, and Remita built in. Customers pay by card or transfer directly from your invoice link.

100% Free & Open Source

No subscription. No hidden fees. Audit every line of code on GitHub. Your data stays yours—always.
